About Us

Businezz Adda is an innovative educational platform that transforms business learning through dynamic, debate-driven experiences. In this unique setup, students represent leading global companies, engaging in debates across various key business domains such as International Press, Business Models, Revenue Strategies, and Emerging Technologies like AI. This platform is designed to cultivate essential leadership skills, deepen participants' understanding of business strategies, and inspire creative problem-solving. By simulating real-world business scenarios, Businezz Adda empowers participants to gain hands-on experience, fostering the development of future entrepreneurs and business leaders. With a focus on both intellectual growth and practical application, this initiative offers a distinctive, engaging approach to business education.
